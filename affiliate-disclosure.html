Affiliate links are special URLs that contain a unique tracking code. When you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the retailer or brand at no additional cost to you. The price you pay remains exactly the same whether you use our affiliate link or go directly to the retailer's website.

Important: You never pay more when using our affiliate links. In fact, we often negotiate exclusive discounts and deals for our readers!

6. FTC Compliance

WoW Glam Decor complies with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines concerning the use of endorsements and testimonials in advertising. The FTC requires that we disclose any material connection between us and the brands or products we recommend.

Key FTC Requirement: When there is an affiliate relationship or any compensation involved, we must clearly and conspicuously disclose this to our readers. This disclosure must be placed where readers are likely to see it before clicking on affiliate links.

We take these regulations seriously and ensure all affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed in compliance with FTC 16 CFR Part 255: "Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising."

7. How Cookies Work with Affiliate Links

When you click on an affiliate link, a small piece of data called a "cookie" is stored in your browser. This cookie helps the retailer track that you came from our website. Here's what you should know:

  • Cookie Duration: Cookies typically last 24 hours to 90 days depending on the affiliate program
  • No Personal Data: Affiliate cookies do not collect personally identifiable information
  • Commission Tracking: They only track that a purchase was made after clicking our link
  • Privacy: The retailer does not share your purchase details with us
  • Control: You can clear cookies from your browser at any time
